Better Lyrics for Youtube Music

Better Lyrics for Youtube Music upgrades your Youtube Music experience by providing beautiful time synced lyrics for the currently playing song.

🖥️ · Steps

Go to Youtube Music, start playing a song and navigate to the lyrics tab. The extension will automatically detect the song and display the lyrics for you.

🛠️ · Features

  • Beautiful time synced lyrics
  • Seek to a specific part of the song by clicking on the lyrics
  • Works with Youtube Music web app without any additional setup
